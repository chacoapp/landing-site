Start Simple

Track casual habits and ambitious goals alike—call your parents, cook something new, or learn a new instrument. Big or small, everything counts.

Get Inspired

Don't have a goal yet? Browse what your friends are doing, find what you like, and dive in! It's easier to stay on track with others by your side.

📸

Share Your Journey

Post updates regularly to showcase your committment! Sharing your progress with others is the best way to keep yourself honest and driven.

💬

Stay Accountable

Friends can like, comment, and vote on the validity of your posts to keep you moving forward. At the end of the day, it's all about support!

🔥

Add Stakes

Set up automated consequences for if you fail—like posting an embarrassing photo or sending an email to a friend. Make it fun, not stressful!

👥

Keep It Real

Chaco isn't about followers or clout. Just invite your close friends, choose who sees each post, and focus on the task at hand.
